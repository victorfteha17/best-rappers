Montana of 300

Walter Anthony Bradford (born March 3, 1989), best known professionally as Montana of 300, is an American hip-hop recording artist from Chicago, Illinois. His rap style is best known for its extensive use of metaphors and complex lyrics set to versatile trap, R&B, and drill beats, while also making remixes to hit songs.
